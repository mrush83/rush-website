HEROES OF BARCARDIA CHARACTER SIGNATURE MOVES

(Base Game)

ABSINTHIA
Between turns, you may swap one of your Power-Ups with a Power-Up that has been dropped in the dungeon.
FLASKIAN
When you disarm a trap, successfully or unsuccessfully, you may pick it up and place it in a different unoccupied room of the dungeon.
INTOXICA
Once during the game, if your HP hits zero, you may roll the die. If you roll an even number, you do not die and instead refill your cup to the 10HP mark.
KEGGAR
When your remaining health is 20HP or less, you receive +2 attack against monsters.
MERLO
Once during the game, on your turn, you may teleport back to the Starting Point no matter where you are in the dungeon.
SIR DRANKALOT
+2 attack in duels, as long as you yell "Huzzah!" before you roll.
